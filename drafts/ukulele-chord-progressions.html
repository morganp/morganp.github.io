30 Great Ukulele Chord Progressions

Published: Mon 08 June 2026
By Morgan Prior

In Music.

tags: ukulele chords music theory progressions

A chord progression is just a sequence of chords that sounds good moving from one to the next. Learn a handful and you can play, or convincingly fake, a huge slice of popular music.

The thirty progressions below come from the lovely Ukulele Go! random progression generator, collected here in one place. Each progression shows its chords as a row of fretboard diagrams you can read left to right.

All diagrams are in standard ukulele tuning, GCEA (low to high).

Where to go next

Pick one progression and loop it until the changes are automatic, then sing a melody over the top. Once a shape lives in your fingers, transposing it to a new key is mostly a matter of swapping in the matching chords. Mix and match the progressions above and you have the raw material for writing your own songs.
