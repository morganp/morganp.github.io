"Matlab: Line Breaks in Strings"

Published: Wed 28 May 2014
By morganp

In posts.

  • Engineering tags:
  • Programming
  • Matlab published: true

Wrap strings with sprintf to allow the \n to be escaped correctly.

>> disp('hello\nworld')
hello\nworld

>> disp(sprintf('hello\nworld'))
hello
world
