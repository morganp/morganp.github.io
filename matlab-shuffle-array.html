"Matlab Shuffle Array"

Published: Thu 06 March 2014
By morganp

In posts.

- Matlab

To shuffle a:

a(randperm(length(a)))

Example:

a = [10 2 5 20];
a = a(randperm(length(a)))

a =

 5    10     2    20
